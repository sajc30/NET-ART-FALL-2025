The Legend

For a thousand years, Saiyans spoke of a legendary warrior who would arise, a Super Saiyan of pure, overwhelming power. Frieza feared this legend so much he destroyed our entire planet to prevent it.

He was right to be afraid.

The golden current is not rage alone; it's the promise I make to my friends. No one else dies today.
