What is a Saiyan?

The Saiyans were a warrior race: proud, fierce, and driven by an instinct to fight. They lived for battle, growing stronger after every defeat. Their home, Planet Vegeta, orbited a red sun and bred warriors who could shake mountains.

But their world was destroyed by the tyrant Frieza, who feared what they might become. Only a handful survived. One of them was a baby named Kakarot.

The Power of Transformation

What makes Saiyans unique is their ability to transform. When pushed to their absolute limits through rage, grief, or sheer will, their bodies unlock new forms. Hair changes color, auras ignite, and power multiplies beyond measure.

Each transformation in this story represents a breakthrough. Not just in strength, but in spirit. They're moments where Goku becomes something more, not for glory, but for the people counting on him.

  • Great Ape — The primal form, triggered by the full moon
  • Super Saiyan — The legendary golden warrior of myth
  • God Forms — Power that rivals divine beings
  • Ultra Instinct — The ultimate state where body moves without thought

The Journey Ahead

You're about to follow Goku through three eras of his life:

  • Dragon Ball — A child with a tail discovers martial arts, friendship, and the beast within
  • Dragon Ball Z — Earth's defender pushes past every limit to protect his home from cosmic threats
  • Dragon Ball Super — Gods take notice. The ceiling keeps rising. Power becomes meditation.
