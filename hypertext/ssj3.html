I found this form in the afterlife, training where time moves differently. When I first show it to Majin Buu, the Earth shakes. Oceans part. The sky darkens under the weight of my aura.

But there's a catch. A big one. This form burns through energy like nothing else. Three minutes feels like three seconds when every heartbeat costs a mountain of stamina.

Transformation Data

  • Traits — Floor-length golden hair, no eyebrows, immense aura
  • Power — 4x SSJ2 (400x base strength)
  • Weakness — Catastrophic stamina drain
  • Use Case — Shock value; short engagements only

The Lesson

SSJ3 taught me something important: raw power isn't the answer. I could be the strongest person in the universe for three minutes and then collapse while my enemy still stands.

The next evolution wouldn't come from pushing harder. It would come from training smarter. From finding power that doesn't burn itself out.

That answer came from the gods themselves...
